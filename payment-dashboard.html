Payment SOP

1. Sent to PFA

Process only verified records in Sent to PFA and confirm status is ready for payout confirmation.

Check customer, PFA, agent, and amount fields before any payment action.

2. Mark Paid

Click Mark Paid only after payment evidence is confirmed by operations.

This updates customer status and makes records available for post-payment settlement tracking.

3. Paid Customers

Use Paid Agents tab to audit each paid agent batch and identify items pending settlement.

Use filters/search to support reconciliation with external payment reports.

4. Settle Commission

Use Settle Commission after commission disbursement is complete to mark records cleared.

Paid means customer payment confirmed; Cleared means commission fully settled.
