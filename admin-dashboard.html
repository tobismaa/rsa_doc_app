Admin SOP

1. User Management

Review Users daily and confirm each account has correct role, status, and contact data.

Deactivate compromised users immediately and log reason in operational records.

2. Pending Users

Approve only staff verified by management. Reject unknown registrations quickly.

Always assign the correct role before activation to prevent access drift.

3. Pending Agents

Open each agent record and verify name, phone, account number, and bank details before approval.

Reject duplicates or suspicious records and notify the uploader to re-register correctly.

4. Registered Agents

Use this tab to audit approved agents and trace who registered and who approved each profile.

Escalate unusual account changes before financial disbursement actions.

5. Application Management: Pending

Monitor newly submitted applications and ensure assignment to active reviewers.

Use comments and audit trail to keep assignment decisions transparent.

6. Application Management: Approved / Rejected

Approved means processing continues to RSA stage; rejected requires clear reason for uploader correction.

Confirm rejected reasons are specific and actionable to reduce rework cycle time.

7. Application Management: Track & Final Submission

Use Track Applications to follow handoff from uploader to reviewer to RSA to payment.

Use Final Submission to verify records sent to PFA and investigate delayed transitions.

8. Application Management: Payment

Mark records paid only after valid payment proof and reconciliation.

Clear paid records only when commission settlement is completed and documented.

9. Round Robin Monitor

Use Reviewer, RSA, and Payment sub-tabs to monitor load distribution.

Reset counters only with authorization and capture reason in audit notes.

10. Audit and Security Practice

Audit Log must be reviewed for unusual actions, frequent role changes, and mass updates.

Keep sensitive data off console/screenshots and enforce least-privilege user access.
